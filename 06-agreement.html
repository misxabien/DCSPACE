Agreement & Data Privacy

Review the policy below before completing your registration.

1 Data Privacy Notice

DC Space collects and processes your personal information, including your student number, school email, course details, and attendance records, solely for academic and campus event management purposes. All data is handled in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (RA 10173) and our institutional privacy guidelines.

  • Data is used only for event tracking and certificate issuance
  • Information is stored securely within institutional systems
  • You may request access or correction of your records at any time

2 Data Sharing & Retention

Your information will not be shared with third parties without your consent, except as required by law. Records are retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill academic and administrative purposes, after which they are securely archived or deleted.

3 Your Rights

Under RA 10173, you have the right to be informed, to access, to object, to erasure or blocking, and to file a complaint with the National Privacy Commission. Contact your school administrator for any privacy-related concerns.
